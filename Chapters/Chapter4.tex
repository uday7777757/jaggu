\section{System Architecture}
The proposed architecture separates the system into data, processing, service, and presentation layers. This improves modularity and makes the solution easier to maintain and extend.

\section{Design Summary}
The design supports modular implementation, staged model development, and future production integration. The use of explicit data flow and UML representations also helps communicate the system clearly to technical and academic reviewers.
